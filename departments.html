Our departments

Specialized care in a serene setting

Four dedicated departments — each with expert teams, modern equipment, and spaces designed to reduce stress.

Department 01

Eye Clinic

Our ophthalmology team delivers comprehensive vision care in naturally lit examination suites. From routine screenings to complex retinal conditions, every visit is unhurried and clearly explained.

  • Digital retinal imaging & glaucoma screening
  • Cataract and LASIK pre-assessment
  • Pediatric and senior eye health programs
  • Low-vision support and prescription updates
Book eye clinic visit

Department 02

Dental Clinic

Gentle dentistry meets modern technology. Our dental suites are designed to feel calm — with sedation options, noise-reducing environments, and family-friendly scheduling.

  • Preventive cleanings and oral health education
  • Restorative, cosmetic, and orthodontic care
  • Same-day emergency dental appointments
  • Children’s first-visit comfort programs
Book dental visit

Department 03

General Consulting / Well-being

Your health journey starts with listening. Our consulting physicians and wellness coaches build personalized plans covering prevention, chronic care, nutrition, and mental well-being.

  • Annual health reviews and lab coordination
  • Chronic disease and medication management
  • Nutrition, fitness, and stress-reduction coaching
  • Referrals to eye, dental, or maternity when needed
Book consultation

Department 04

Maternity

From your first antenatal visit through delivery and beyond, our maternity team provides compassionate, evidence-based care in private, homelike birthing suites.

  • Antenatal classes and ultrasound scheduling
  • Labour, delivery, and recovery suites
  • Lactation consulting and newborn checks
  • Postnatal mental health and family support
Book maternity visit
