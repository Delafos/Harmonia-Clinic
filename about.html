FAQ

Questions we hear often

What should I bring to my first visit?

Photo ID, insurance card if applicable, medication list, and completed online registration. Maternity patients should bring prior scan records if available.

Can I visit multiple departments in one day?

Yes. Mention all departments when booking and we will align your schedule to minimize waiting.

Do you offer maternity tours?

We offer monthly suite tours — contact us or book through general consulting to reserve a spot.

How do I cancel or reschedule?

Call the front desk or use the link in your confirmation email. 24 hours notice helps us offer your slot to others.
